FAQ
Common questions

Everything Tasmanian business owners ask us before getting started.

How long does a website take to build? +

A standard business website takes 2–4 weeks from sign-off to launch. An online store typically takes 3–6 weeks. We give you a firm timeline in your proposal and stick to it.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to work with you? +

Not at all. We explain everything in plain English and make sure you can manage your own website after handover. If you ever get stuck, just call us — we'll sort it out.

Can you improve my existing website? +

Yes. We can improve, redesign, or add features to an existing site. Just share the link and we'll give you an honest assessment of what's worth keeping and what needs to change.

What's included in the monthly marketing retainer? +

Our retainers include ad account management (Google and/or Facebook), monthly reporting, ongoing SEO work, and regular strategy reviews. Ad spend is billed separately directly through the platforms so you always see exactly where your money goes.

Do you only work with Hobart businesses? +

Not at all — we work with businesses all across Tasmania, including Launceston, Devonport, Burnie, and regional areas. Most work happens over video call, but we're happy to meet face-to-face for Hobart clients.

What happens after my website launches? +

We hand over full ownership with a walkthrough so you know how to make simple changes yourself. We also offer ongoing maintenance and support plans if you'd rather leave the technical side to us. Either way, we're always contactable.
